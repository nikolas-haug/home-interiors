About

Our interior products planner is a simple app to help you plan your customized interior needs.

Selecting items for your home interior has never been easier! Our app makes it easy to select products for your home interior. Currently you can select doors, kitchens and wooden flooring using this app. Sign-up to select a variety of interior products. Save your interior package selections and share them with your family members and contractors. This app will let you update and delete items in your interior package. Finish selecting your items to see the estimated total package price. Start your journey with us!
